What is KICK?

KICK is generated through bet-mining. The KICK token will be a Tron compliant token built on top of the Tron blockchain, defined by an open-source smart contract. The total number of tokens generated is 100,000,000. There will be no more tokens generated after this amount.

How can I get KICK ?

KICK can be gained just by playing games on the TRONStrike platform. Every wager you make, win or lose, you will earn KICK. The more you wager, the more you will receive. Holders can claim rewards from the dividends pot by freezing their KICK tokens on the website to receive rewards every 48 hours.

What is KITZ?

KITZ token is created with the Total Supply of 3 Billion for the purpose of being used as a FUEL on TRONStrike platform, KITZ tokens will be used to purchase in-game items in our incoming multi-player games and a fraction of the profit generated through the sales of in-game items will be destroyed every month.

Platform Dividends

Dividends representing 40% of the platform revenue will be invested in the dividend pool to reward players, and users can freeze their KICK tokens to earn dividends. The amount of dividends entitled every 48 hours is proportional to the amount of KICK you freeze.

KICK can be earned by playing any game on TRONStrike. The overall mining progress is split in to 10 stage with each stage having a total of 10,000,000 KICK released. The detailed information is outlined in the whitepaper.
